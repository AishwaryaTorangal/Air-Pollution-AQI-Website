Why Air Quality Matters

Air pollution is one of the greatest environmental risks to human health. Poor air quality can lead to respiratory diseases, heart conditions, and other serious health issues.

Our platform provides real-time Air Quality Index (AQI) data and historical and future trends to help you make informed decisions about your outdoor activities and protect your health.

Understanding AQI Scale

0-50: Good - Air quality is satisfactory
51-100: Moderate - Acceptable quality
101-150: Unhealthy for Sensitive Groups
151-200: Unhealthy - Health effects possible
201-300: Very Unhealthy - Health alert
301+: Hazardous - Emergency conditions
